About me


I was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I was raised in Rio by a fantastic and loving family! During my life there, I made many friends that I still keep until these days. I left Brazil to move to USA over 20 year ago. My first house in the US was Washington D.C., and subsequently I lived in NV, CA and now, TX is were I call home.


I started my career as a software developer. I decided to move to the business side and became a project/program manager. I always missed my technical life, so I decided to re-learn coding and change careers. I am studying to become web developer and I am loving every minute of my course!


I love the outdoors, especially the beach! I used to be a scuba diver and dove with sharks. I love traveling, reading and watching movies!
